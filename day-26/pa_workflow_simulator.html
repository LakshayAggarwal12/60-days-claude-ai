📋 How to Use This Simulator

Drag patient case cards through the workflow stages, from left to right across three lanes. Patient Lane: Intake → Insurance Verify → Document Collection. Click any card for a detailed educational walkthrough and guided action buttons. Start by clicking New Patient Case above!
